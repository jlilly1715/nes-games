Lunar Limit
by Pubby

An early 80s arcade shooter for the NES. Play it on an emulator or NES hardware!
Lunar Limit puts you in control of a miniature version of planet earth, "Mini Earth", and your faithful satellite, "The Moon Cannon", in an all-out-war against evil Atari invaders.
