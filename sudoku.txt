Sudoku - NESWORLD Edition
by Al Bailey

Released in 2007 by Al Bailey. First complete NES game published in years.
Al even went ahead and created a limited set of gold and grey Sudoku carts which were available for purchase on several expos in the US and Canada.
Lately it has been available at www.retrousb.com for the low price os US$20. This also makes Sudoku the first massproduced NES game in years.
